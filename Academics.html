Curriculum

Ordinally Level

At Hillstone School, the ordinally level(O-Level) curriculum is designed to provide students with a strong academic foundation, critical thinking skills, and practical knowledge to prepare them for Advanced Level(A-level) studies and future careers.

Subjects offered

Students study a balanced mix of Arts and Sciences, ensuring a well-rounded education. Core subjects typically include:

  • Mathematics: Developing problem solving and analytical skills.
  • English Language & Literature: Enhancing communication and comprehension abilities.
  • Humanities (History & Political Education, Geography, Religious Education): Expanding understanging of society and global perspectives.
  • Science (Biology, Chemistry, Physics): Encouraging inqury, experimentation, and discovery.
  • Entrepreneurship: Preparing students for financiak literacy and real-world decision-making.
  • ICT(Information & Communicatio Technology): Building digital literacy and technological competence.

Skills-Based Learning & Vocation Training

Alongside traditional academics, students at Hillstone School engage in vocational training, equiping them with markatable skills for future success, These include:

  • Taoloring: Hands-on experience in fashion and textiles.
  • Welding & Metal Fabrication: Technical training in industrial work.
  • Construction & Building: practical knowledge for future careers in engineering and architecture.
  • Performing Arts: Fostering creativity in music, dance and drama.

Assessment & Examinations

Students undergo continuous assessment through coursework, practicals and examinations to ensure a comprehensive understanding of subjects. At the end of the O-Level cycle, they sit for national examinations, paving the way for A-level studies, vocational careers, or entrepreneurship.
