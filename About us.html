School Profile

Hillstone High school is a private, sponsored insitution established to provide quality education, vocational training, and strong ethical foundaations. Rooted in Anglican principles, we embrace diversity, discipline, and academic excellence, welcoming students from all backgrounds.

Hillstone High School offers Ordinary(O) and Advanced (A) level education, with a cirriculum that balances Arts and Sciences to prepare students for higher education careers.

We instill in our students:

  • Discipline: Encourage responsible and focus.
  • Smartness: Maintaining neatness and readiness for success.
  • Respect: Fostering care for self, others, and
  • school property.
  • Fear of the God: Nurturind spirituality and moral responsibility.
  • Academic Excellence: Striving for the highest intellectual achievement.
